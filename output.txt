## Relationships

### Cart Relationships

- **BelongsTo** `user` to User (Local Key: `user_id`, Foreign Key: `id`)
- **HasMany** `items` to CartItem (Local Key: `id`, Foreign Key: `cart_id`)

### CartItem Relationships

- **BelongsTo** `cart` to Cart (Local Key: `cart_id`, Foreign Key: `id`)
- **BelongsTo** `product` to Product (Local Key: `product_id`, Foreign Key: `id`)

### Order Relationships

- **BelongsTo** `user` to User (Local Key: `user_id`, Foreign Key: `id`)
- **HasMany** `items` to OrderItem (Local Key: `id`, Foreign Key: `order_id`)
- **BelongsTo** `paymentMethod` to PaymentMethod (Local Key: `payment_method_id`, Foreign Key: `id`)
- **BelongsTo** `promotion` to Promotion (Local Key: `promotion_id`, Foreign Key: `id`)
- **HasOne** `payment` to Payment (Local Key: `id`, Foreign Key: `order_id`)
- **BelongsTo** `shippingCostConfirmedBy` to User (Local Key: `shipping_cost_confirmed_by`, Foreign Key: `id`)
- **BelongsTo** `stockRestoredBy` to User (Local Key: `stock_restored_by`, Foreign Key: `id`)

### OrderItem Relationships

- **BelongsTo** `order` to Order (Local Key: `order_id`, Foreign Key: `id`)
- **BelongsTo** `product` to Product (Local Key: `product_id`, Foreign Key: `id`)

### Payment Relationships

- **BelongsTo** `order` to Order (Local Key: `order_id`, Foreign Key: `id`)
